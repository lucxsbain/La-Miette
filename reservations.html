We are located 57 Toorak Road West, South Yarra. The Bar Menu is available daily from noon onwards for walk-in only.

Our early sittings have a maximum of two hours - therefore if you are wanting a longer sitting time we suggest you book a later time slot.

Our children's menu is available for children under 12. Due to our limited space we are unable to accommodate prams.

We do not allow any outside food or beverage to be consumed on our premises. You can order one of our Celebration Cakes with at least 48 hours notice before your reservation.

A 10% surcharge applies on weekends, a 15% surcharge applies on all public holidays. Card transactions incur a 1.5% surcharge.
